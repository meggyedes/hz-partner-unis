About HZ University of Applied Sciences

Located in the beautiful province of Zeeland in the Netherlands, HZ University of Applied Sciences has been consistently ranked in the top three Dutch multidisciplinary universities of applied sciences since 2011. With around 4,800 students, we focus on practice-based education and research, maintaining close collaborations with the business world and research centers.
